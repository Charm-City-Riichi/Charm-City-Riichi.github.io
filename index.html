Charm City Riichi is a thriving community of riichi mahjong players in Baltimore and Savage. We meet weekly for in-person games and welcome players of all skill levels—from complete newcomers to experienced competitors.

Riichi mahjong is a competitive four-player tile game that combines strategy and luck in equal measure. There's enough depth to keep things interesting, but also enough chance that anyone can win on any given night. You'll meet cool people and end up way more invested than you'd expect.

No prior experience needed. We provide all the tiles, teach you as you go, and guide you through table etiquette.
