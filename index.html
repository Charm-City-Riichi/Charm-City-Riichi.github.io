Charm City Riichi


A thriving community of riichi mahjong players in Maryland. We meet multiple times a week for in-person games in Baltimore and Savage!

Riichi mahjong is a competitive four-player tile game that combines strategy and luck.

Players of all skill levels, from complete newcomers to experienced competitors, are welcome here.
