One-Day Events

When & Where

New in 2026, we are hosting larger events once a quarter! In spring, fall, and winter, we're holding one-day tournaments.

  • Spring: March 7th, 2026
  • Summer: (Two-day tournament) June 20-21
  • Fall: TBD
  • Winter: TBD

These events are hosted at the Omnihedral board game store, located in Maryland between Baltimore and DC.

The street address is 8600 Foundry St, Savage, MD 20763.

Ruleset & Format

  • 32 player cap
  • 5 games
  • Pure scramble (players assigned to tables randomly)
  • No elimination (all players play all five games)
  • 75+0 minute timer (if game is still going when timer finishes, players finish their current hand)
  • Lower penalties
