Charm City Riichi Open 2026

When & Where

June 20-21, 2026.

CCRO '26 will be held at Omnihedral. Street address: 8600 Foundry St, Savage, MD 20763

We have not made arrangements with local hotels, but there are many options along the Route 1 and Route 32 corridors.

Accessibility Note: There are dedicated parking spaces for people with disabilities and a ramp we can use that leads to an alternate entrance, but please contact the tournament organizers if you are intending to use these so we can ensure a staff member is there to help out.

Registration

Registration will open in April. Details will be added to this webpage & will be posted to our Discord server.

32 player cap (likely to expand to 48 players before registration opens).

Entry fee TBD, expected to be below $60.

Prizes TBD.

Ruleset & Format

  • WRC 2025
  • Lower penalties
  • Nagashi mangan
  • 15/5 uma
  • Atamahane nashi (No Head-bump!)
  • 10 hanchan scramble, top 4 cut for final two rounds*
  • 75 minutes + 0 per hanchan.

*Top 4 players are locked after 8 rounds and will play on a final table of 2 rounds with their tournament points reset to 0. Everyone else will continue their last 2 rounds as usual. All participants will play a complete 10 rounds over the course of the event.
