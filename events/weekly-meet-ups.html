Weekly Meet-Ups

Baltimore: No Land Beyond

We meet twice a week at No Land Beyond, a board game store and bar in Baltimore, Maryland (street address 108 W North Ave, Baltimore, MD 21201).

  • Thursdays: 6 – 10pm
  • Sundays: 1 – 5pm

No Land Beyond reserves a set number of tables for our club. Please sign up through Eventbrite!

Savage: Omnihedral

We meet once a week at Omnihedral, a board game store in Savage, Maryland (street address 8600 Foundry St, Savage, MD 20763).

  • Tuesdays: 6 – 10pm
