Score Trainer

Practice scoring riichi mahjong hands. Each hand is presented with the full context you'd see at the table — round wind, seat, dora, calls, riichi status, and the winning tile. Work out the han, fu, and payment, then check your answer.

Hands are shown in standard notation: digits followed by suit letter, where m = man (characters), p = pin (dots), s = sou (bamboo), z = honors (1–4 = E/S/W/N, 5–7 = white/green/red dragons). A 0 in m, p, or s denotes a red five.
